HTTPS clone URL: https://github.com/Exherbo/desktop-unofficial.git

To move to the new repository location in your Paludis configuration,
please change /etc/paludis/repositories/desktop-unofficial.conf's "sync ="
entry from git://github.com/codejunky/desktop-unofficial.git to 
git://github.com/Exherbo/desktop-unofficial.git.

Existing contributors were added to the new location.
